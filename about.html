About Space Quiz Adventure

Welcome to Space Quiz Adventure! Embark on thrilling missions across the solar system. Select your destination, prepare your rocket, pass the pre-flight checks, and navigate the cosmos. Collect valuable samples from distant worlds and test your knowledge along the way.

Technology Used

This project primarily utilizes core web technologies:

  • HTML: For structuring the content and game screens.
  • CSS: For styling the user interface, creating visual effects, and ensuring responsiveness.
  • JavaScript: For game logic, interactivity, state management (using `localStorage`), and dynamic content updates.
